Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 25, 2026

Mexico City Explorer ("the App") is developed by Alberto Rodriguez Salgado. This policy describes how the App handles your information.

Data Collection

The App does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers. All data remains on your device.

Location Data

The App uses your device's location services to:

  • Show nearby points of interest on the map
  • Detect when you arrive at landmarks to offer audio guides
  • Calculate distances to points of interest

Location data is processed entirely on your device and is never sent to any server or third party.

Purchases

The App offers a one-time premium purchase processed entirely through Apple's App Store. We do not have access to your payment information.

Third-Party Services

The App does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Data Storage

The App stores your preferences (such as visited places and settings) locally on your device using standard iOS storage. This data is not accessible to us or any third party.

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at the email address listed on the App Store.
